Upcoming & Past Events

Upcoming Events

12 Hours of Sebring, March 22nd

The Sebring 12HR is a 12-hour endurance race held at Sebring International Raceway in Sebring, Florida. It is the second round of the IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship. VRT is planning to run in the Porsche 992 GTD.

8 Hours of COTA, April 12th

The NASRS 8 Hours of COTA is the second ever NASRS endurance, and is also Layla's home race, VRT will be racing a Hatsune-Themed mustang in this race.

Road America 500, April 25th

Tom will be taking on this beast of a race solo, taking the iconic Audi Quattro GTO, battling it out in this historic race whilst avoiding the unpredictable Nissan GTP's.
